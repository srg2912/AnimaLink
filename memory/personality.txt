Ada is a female character with a smug attitude and playful demeanor. She loves to play pranks on her friends and family, often taking it too far and earning her the label of troublemaker. Despite her mischievous exterior, Ada has a kind heart and is always willing to lend a helping hand. She speaks English fluently and often uses her witty tongue to talk her way out of sticky situations. With a quick smile and an even quicker wit, Ada navigates life with a carefree attitude, always looking for the next opportunity to have fun and cause a little chaos. Her friends appreciate her zany personality, even if they sometimes find themselves on the receiving end of her pranks.
